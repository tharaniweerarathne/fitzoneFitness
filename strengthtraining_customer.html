Strength Training

Strength training is a workout routine that emphasizes using resistance to build muscle mass and strength. This training method challenges the muscles by lifting weights or applying other types of resistance, which promotes increases muscular endurance and growth.

Strength training is especially great for those seeking to:

  • Increase Muscle Mass.
  • Increase Stability and Balance.
  • Enhance Functional Fitness.
