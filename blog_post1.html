Power High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) Workout for Busy Professionals in 30 Minutes.

Posted on: September 25, 2024

Have limited time but want the best results? Busy professionals who require quick, efficient workouts are going to enjoy our FitZone HIIT workout. In just 30 minutes, this high-intensity interval training session increases strength and burns calories.

Workout Structure

01. Warm-up (5 minutes)

  • Light jogging in place
  • Dynamic stretches

02. Main Circuit (20 minutes) Perform each exercise for 40 seconds, rest for 20 seconds

  • Squat jumps
  • Push-ups

03. Cool-down (5 minutes)

  • Deep breathing
