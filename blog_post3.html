Your Guide for Creating Successful Custom Meal Plans.

Posted on: October 6, 2024

FitZone's professionally designed meal plans may transform your fitness journey. We provide nutritionist-approved meal plans that go well with your workout routine, whether your goal is to maintain a healthy lifestyle, gain muscle, or lose weight.

Recognizing your nutritional needs.

Let's first review FitZone's basic nutrition principles before getting into specific meal plans:

  • Protein: 0.8-1.2g per pound of body weight
  • Carbohydrates: 45-65% of daily calories
  • Hydration: Minimum 8-10 glasses of water daily

7-Day Meal Plan for Losing Weight

Breakfast:

  • Egg white omelet with spinach
  • Black coffee
  • Whole grain toast

Mid-Morning:

  • Protein smoothie with berries

Lunch:

  • Grilled chicken salad

Dinner:

  • Steamed vegetables
  • Cauliflower rice
