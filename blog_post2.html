At FitZone Fitness Center, we understand that consuming nutritious foods is crucial to reaching your best potential. Today, we're thrilled to present some delicious recipes that have been approved by nutritionists and will help fuel your workouts and aid in your recovery!

Pre-Workout Power Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder

Blend all the ingredients together until they are smooth. This smoothie is high in potassium and offers long-lasting energy, making it ideal for avoiding muscle cramps while working out.
